Ashenafi Mulugeta

Ashenafi has worked for various government and private organizations on different capacities. He served as export promotions officer at Ethiopian Customs Authority and was directly responsible for promoting export trade from Ethiopia by providing export trade incentive schemes to manufacturers and exporters. He worked as member of the national standard input output coefficient committee for 4 years.

Ashenafi also worked for various international companies as commercial manager, international trade expert and advisor as well as marketing and procurement manager at different times before he joined the academic world as a lecturer.

He served Addis Ababa Univeristy, Faculty of Business and Economics for 5 years as lecturer in public administration, public finance, public policy, political science, public personnel management, research writing etc while at the same time worked as research advisor for many undergraduate students. Ashenafi was elected by students as the best lecturer for consecutive 3 years from 2008 up to 2010.

He founded and co-founded 10 companies after having resigned from his lecturer position from Addis Ababa University. His startups are currently active in logistics, transport, multimedia and international trading as well as innovation and technology incubation and acceleration businesses. Just recently, he oversaw the foundation and operation of a continental platform called African Innovation Week that was organized by one of his companies IBA Ethiopia Center for Innovation, African Union and Ministry of Innovation and Technology, the first of its kind to be organized at continental level.

Ashenafi is currently active in setting up youth entrepreneurship development centers in various universities in Ethiopia in collaboration with Ministry of Innovation and Technology as well as a national center of excellence for innovation and entrepreneurship in collaboration with Ministry of Science and Higher Education.

Ashenafi possesses BA degree in business management from Jimma University and MBA degree in public administration and development management from Addis Ababa University. He also possesses post graduate diploma in procurement and supply chain management from International Trade Center as well as certificate from Ethiopian Customs Authority on customs agency and clearance of international goods. He is also a qualified trainer in supply chain management and procurement from International Trade Center.

He is one of the co-producers of the Man-Ke-Man Show that used to be broadcast on EBS TV on weekly basis.
