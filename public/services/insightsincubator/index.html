Insights Incubator

Insight Incubators (II) incubates and accelerates innovative ideas from different walks of life especially from young high school students, university students, university graduates and community, research institutions, government organizations, rural community.

Africa is the youngest continent in terms of age of its population. The case for Ethiopia, which is the 2nd most populated nation in the continent, is not different. More than 65% of its population is less than 35 years of age. On top of this, the rate of unemployment especially in urban areas is more than 25%. The economy of the country is not capable of absorbing the young educated manpower due to its limited vibrancy and lack of enterprise. Thus, increasing the capacity of young minds to create their own job opportunities is critical.

The incubation program includes gathering innovative ideas, early phase incubation of these innovative ideas, business proposal writing, shaping marketing strategies, devising financing strategies, going into market, mass production and continuous business consultancy and support.
