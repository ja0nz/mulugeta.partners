Ethio-Bayern Chamber of Commerce

Bavaria is the debatably the strongest state in Germany in terms of economy. Various industries zare based in this state. World famous brands such as Mercedes and BMW are from Bavaria. Therefore, seeking flow of investment from Germany shall no doubt require attracting investment from the Bavaria state.

On the other hand, Ethiopia is largely liberalizing many of its sectors including telecom, power, banking and transport etc. Owing to predominant command economic structures that prevailed for almost half a century, the share the private sector in its economy is very limited. Ethio-Bayern Chamber of Commerce (EBCC) aspires to connect investors from the Bavarian state of Germany with that of investors in Ethiopia; attract the business community of Bavaria to Ethiopia; promote import of Ethiopian commodities largely dominated by coffee, skins and hides, flower and herbs; promote export of German products largely dominated by industrial products such as machineries, electronic items, computers and other IT equipment, cars and locomotives; expand partnerships between German and Ethiopian businesses in services such as consultancy in agriculture, architecture, construction etc;
